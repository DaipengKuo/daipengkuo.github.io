About

I am a Ph.D. student in Intelligent Mechatronic Engineering at Xi'an Technological University. My research focuses on affective computing, computer vision, facial expression recognition, and multimodal sentiment analysis. I work on robust perception under uncontrolled conditions, including illumination variation, pose changes, occlusion, temporal noise, and imbalanced modal contributions.

My recent work explores action-unit-guided attention, spatio-temporal transformers, contrastive learning, multi-scale temporal modeling, and multimodal fusion for facial expression analysis.
